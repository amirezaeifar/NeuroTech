## 1. Core Aesthetic (Minimalist & Evolutionary)
The primary design language is minimalist, luxury, and formal, but intentionally flexible to accommodate future UI evolutions.
*   **Baseline Style:** Default to a clean, elegant aesthetic relying on solid colors, clear borders, and high-quality typography.
*   **Controlled Depth & Effects:** Shadows (`box-shadow`), subtle gradients, and depth effects are permitted. When applying them, ensure they enhance the premium feel and do not clutter the interface.
*   **Hover States:** Interactions should feel alive and premium. You may use soft shadow elevations, gentle scaling, or color transitions, provided they maintain a cohesive luxury theme.
*   **Visual Hierarchy:** Maintain hierarchy primarily through typography and spacing (Tailwind's scale), but feel free to introduce structural depth (like lifted cards, soft glows, or layered elements) when it improves the user experience.
